About Porta Da Lua

The only hotel right on the beach

Located directly on the beach in Praia do Forte, Bahia, Porta Da Lua is a unique seaside hotel known for its beautiful tropical architecture and peaceful atmosphere. With its premier beach front most offering stunning ocean and reef views guests can relax to the soothing sea breeze in one of the most privileged locations in the region.

Just a short walk from the lively village center, the hotel offers the perfect balance between tranquility and access to shops, restaurants, and afterlife. Whether enjoy a cocktail on the beach or dining in your suite, our dedicated team ensure a warm, welcoming, and memorable stay.

Designed by renowned Brazilian architect Wilson Reis Netto, Porto Da Lua is considered a masterpiece of tropical architecture. Fully renovated for modern comfort while preserving its natural ventilation and charm, the hotel combines elegance, history, and authenticity.

Charming like a pousada, with the comfort of a hotel -- Porto da Lua offers the best of both worlds.
