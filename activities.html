Activities

Discover unforgettable experiences just steps from our door

What to do in Praia do Forte

Our corner of Bahia offers a wealth of natural wonders and cultural experiences. Whether you're looking for adventure or tranquillity, we've curated the best local activities for our guests.

Must See

Projeto TAMAR

Brazil's pioneering sea turtle conservation centre, right in Praia do Forte. Watch hatchlings, learn about marine biology, and see rescued turtles up close.

5 min walk from hotel
Visit Website
Historic

Castelo Garcia D'Ávila

The only medieval-style castle in the Americas. Explore the ruins of this 16th-century Portuguese fortress perched on a hilltop with panoramic ocean views.

10 min drive
Visit Website
Seasonal

Whale Watching

From July to November, humpback whales migrate to the warm waters of Bahia. Take a guided boat tour with the Instituto Baleia Jubarte for an unforgettable encounter.

Praia do Forte harbour
Visit Website

Natural Pools & Snorkelling

At low tide, crystal-clear natural pools form along the reef, creating a perfect spot for snorkelling among tropical fish and starfish. Equipment available at our front desk.

Right on our beach
Learn More

Horseback Riding

Ride along the stunning coastline at sunset on horseback. Guided tours take you through coconut groves, river crossings, and empty stretches of white sand beach.

Arranged via front desk
Learn More
Nature

Reserva Sapiranga

600 hectares of protected Atlantic Forest with hiking trails, kayaking on the Pojuca River, and zip-lining through the canopy. A true eco-adventure.

15 min drive
